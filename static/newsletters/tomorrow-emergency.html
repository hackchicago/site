What you must bring with you

  • ​​​​A student or government ID. If you do not bring an ID, security will not let you into the venue.
  • ​​​​​​A laptop + charger. What you may want to bring with you

  • ​​​​A sleeping bag and toiletries. We’ll have a designated venue sleeping area if you want to sleep, but it’s up to you to bring what you need to make yourself comfortable.
  • ​​A power strip/USB hub if you’re bringing many devices with you. There are limited power outlets, and we can’t provide many extra strips.
  • ​​​​​​​​A water bottle. We’ll have soda and limited bottled water, but plan to refill your water bottles at the sinks.

Details about arriving tomorrow

  • ​​​​​​Windy City Hacks will be held at Origami Risk, which is located in the Loop next to the Chicago River. You can find the address and more info about our venue here: windyhacks.com/venue
  • ​​Doors are opening earlier now—10:30am!—and check-in ends at 11:50am. Please make sure to arrive during this window. Opening ceremony starts at 12pm.

Late registrations & anyone who did not RSVP will be on a waitlist, and admitted if there are still spots available. We’re sorry we can’t let everyone in—we want to!—but we’ve had enormous interest in the event and the venue has a fire code limit.
